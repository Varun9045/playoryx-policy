PlayOryX Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data PlayOryX collects, how we use it, and your choices. It applies to the PlayOryX mobile game and related multiplayer features.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the game, we collect:

  • Account data from Firebase Authentication (Email, Google, or Anonymous sign-in)
  • Player profile information you create in the game
  • Gameplay and multiplayer activity data
  • In-game chat data
  • Device and diagnostic information
  • Rewarded ad interaction data

Gameplay Data

We use Firebase Realtime Database to store gameplay data such as player profiles, coins, XP, rewards, match participation, and progression. This data is needed to run game features, sync progress across sessions, and support multiplayer play.

Chat Data

PlayOryX includes multiplayer chat. Chat messages and related metadata (for example sender, room, and time) may be processed and stored to deliver chat, detect abuse, enforce rules, and keep players safe.

Please do not share sensitive personal information in chat.

Device Information

We may collect technical information such as:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version, language, and region/country (approximate)
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics
  • Security and fraud-prevention signals

Advertising Data

PlayOryX uses rewarded ads only. Ads are shown only when you choose to watch one in exchange for an in-game benefit.

We and our ad partners may process ad event data such as ad request, ad impression, ad completion, and reward grant status to provide and measure rewarded ads.

How We Use Data

  • Provide authentication, gameplay, multiplayer, and chat features
  • Save progress, profiles, coins, XP, and rewards
  • Moderate chat, prevent cheating, fraud, and abuse
  • Maintain app security, stability, and performance
  • Operate rewarded ads and deliver earned rewards
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that process data on our behalf, including:

  • Google Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Realtime Database
  • Rewarded advertising providers

These providers process data under their own privacy terms. We recommend reviewing their policies.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in ways that violate applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information without proper consent, contact us and we will investigate and take appropriate action.

Account Deletion

You can request account and data deletion by contacting support.playoryx@gmail.com.

We will review and process valid deletion requests within a reasonable time, unless we must retain certain data for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or dispute-resolution reasons.

If you use Anonymous sign-in and uninstall the game before linking your account, some data may not be recoverable.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share data only:

  • With service providers needed to operate the game
  • To comply with legal obligations or lawful requests
  • To protect users, enforce policies, and prevent fraud or abuse
  • In a merger, acquisition, or business transfer with appropriate safeguards

Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here and change the effective date above.

Contact Information

For privacy questions or requests, contact: support.playoryx@gmail.com
