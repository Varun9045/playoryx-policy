Delete Your PlayOryX Account

If you would like to delete your PlayOryX account and associated data, you can do so using one of the methods below:

Method 1 — In-App Deletion

Open the PlayOryX app → Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.

Method 2 — Request via Email

Email us at support.playoryx@gmail.com from your registered email address with the subject "Delete My Account".

We will process your request and permanently delete your account and associated personal data within a reasonable time.

Data Deleted

  • Account information
  • Profile data
  • Gameplay progress
  • Messages associated with your account

Some anonymous or aggregated data may be retained for analytics and security purposes.
